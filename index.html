Our Vision

The vision of umu Igbo Brandon Association inc is to be a model sociocultural association committed to promoting Igbo cultural values, norms, heritage, language, tradition and language within the city of Brandon, Manitoba.

Our Mission

Rooted in the Igbo ethics of hardwork, integrity, mentorship, respect for self and others, the mission of the Umu Igbo Brandon Association Inc. is to achieve its vision through dedicated cultural, educational, charitable, social, and exonomic programs and activities.

Our Objectives

  • Promote Igbo Language, Values and Culture
  • Advance the Igbo Community interests in the City of Brandon, the province of Manitoba and Canada.
  • Promote the Welfare of Members of Umu Igbo Brandon
  • Assist new Igbo New-comers to settle seamlessly in Brandon
  • Promote cultural diversity and understanding through active cooperation with other cultural associations in the city of Brandon, Province of Manitoba and Canada
  • Undertake cultural, charitable, social and economic activities beneficial to members and the society at large.
