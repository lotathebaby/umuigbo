Our Foundation

Built on the pillars of culture, community, and progress

Our Vision

To be a model sociocultural association committed to promoting Igbo cultural values, norms, heritage, language, and traditions within Brandon, Manitoba.

Our Mission

Rooted in Igbo ethics of hard work, integrity, mentorship, and respect, we achieve our vision through dedicated cultural, educational, charitable, social, and economic programs.

Our Objectives

• Promote Igbo Language, Values and Culture

• Advance community interests in Brandon and Manitoba

• Promote member welfare and support

• Assist new Igbo newcomers in settling

• Foster cultural diversity and cooperation

• Organize beneficial community activities
